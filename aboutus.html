Non-Emergency Medical Transportation

We have enertered the NEMT space as service providers under a rideshare business model. We are starting this startup as a service to support patients and their relatives during their hard times. Transportation of patients who need access to medical facilities from either far off locations, or from nearby locations but have a problem in transporting the patient to the medical facilities is termed broadly as non- emergency medical transportation.


Our Solution

Welcab NEMT service provide affordable, easily available, reliable and patient care service for our citizens. We provide call based taxi dispatch system, When patient and relatives makes call for booking a WELCAB, the contact centre executive ask for pickup-point and destination which is passed to the Taxi Dispach System. The TDS instantly locates. near by taxi driver, send trip information and on approval from the taxi drivers sends confirmation note to the customer.
